About AquaMesh

AquaMesh is a premium tile company specializing in modern, traditional designs and professional installation services. We work with residential and commercial clients to bring clean, durable, and elegant tile solutions to life. AquaMesh has revolutionized the tile landscape, our treated limestone tile is designed specifically to drain standing water out of your bathroom floor, eliminating the risk of slipping and the annoyance of cold standing water.
